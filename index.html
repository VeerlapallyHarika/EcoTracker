Green Living Tips üåø

Science-backed sustainability recommendations to reduce your environmental impact.

Energy Efficiency

Switch to LED bulbs (80% less energy), unplug devices when not in use, and use programmable thermostats. This can reduce energy emissions by 30-50%.

üö¥

Green Transportation

Cycle, walk, or use public transit when possible. Electric vehicles produce 50% fewer emissions than gasoline cars over their lifetime.

üçÉ

Plant-Based Diet

Eating meat produces 5-10x more emissions than plant foods. Swap 2-3 meals per week for vegetarian options to significantly reduce your footprint.

‚ôªÔ∏è

Waste Reduction

Follow the 3Rs: Reduce consumption, Reuse items, and Recycle properly. Composting food waste prevents methane emissions from landfills.

üíß

Water Conservation

Install low-flow showerheads, fix leaks, and reduce water heating. Saving water saves energy and reduces wastewater treatment emissions.

Renewable Energy

Switch to renewable energy plans or install solar panels. Renewables now account for 46% of global capacity and are the fastest-growing energy source.

üõçÔ∏è

Conscious Shopping

Buy secondhand, choose sustainable brands, and avoid fast fashion. One new outfit's production emits ~7 kg CO‚ÇÇ. Thrifting saves 2-7 kg per item.

üå≥

Support Reforestation

Trees absorb ~20 kg CO‚ÇÇ over their lifetime. Plant native trees, support forest conservation, or invest in verified carbon offset projects.

üì±

Digital Footprint

Reduce data storage (each email stored = emissions), limit video streaming, and delete old files. Data centers consume 3% of global electricity.
